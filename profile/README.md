# Axioma 🍳

Axioma is building an AI-powered meal planning and grocery execution platform.

Our system transforms food intent into structured recipes, ingredient lists, and retailer-ready shopping carts.

## Product

**Chef** is our core application:

food idea → recipe → constraints → missing ingredients → grocery cart

We are currently integrating retailer APIs to enable users to seamlessly move from planning meals to purchasing ingredients.

## Goal

Reduce friction between deciding what to cook and actually getting the ingredients.

## Status

In active development.
